About Jen;
Jen is a two-spirit indigenous person from Treaty 1, Winnipeg, Canada. (They/Them)
Jen is 28 and has 3 siblings. They spent their life dedicating their time to their family.
Their passion in life is family as well as technology.
They believe in human rights as well as LGBT2S rights. They hope with their new skills in tech, they can try to make small a difference in the world.
